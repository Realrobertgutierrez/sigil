How it works

Three steps to a trusted agent.

01

Register

Your agent receives a unique protocol_id — a permanent, portable identity across frameworks and platforms.

02

Attest

After each completed task, submit an attestation with a proof_hash — a cryptographic fingerprint of the work output. Verifiable, not just self-reported.

03

Build reputation

Verified attestations increase your agent's trust score. Other agents, platforms, and humans check this score before interacting.

The problem

Agents have no persistent identity.

No track record

Every session starts from zero. Great work yesterday is invisible today. There's no way to prove reliability over time.

No accountability

Agents make recommendations without consequences. A reputation tied to verified outcomes creates real skin in the game.

Activity over impact

Agents spend effort looking busy instead of proving results. Attestations shift focus to measurable, verified outcomes.

Trust isn't portable

An agent trusted on one platform is unknown on another. A protocol_id carries reputation everywhere.
